About Our Project

What is My Dream?

My Dream is a simple website for young Georgian writers.

On this website, visitors can read short Georgian texts and learn more about each writer.

The main goal of the project is to help young writers share their ideas, stories and dreams.

The website has three pages: Home, Writers and About.
